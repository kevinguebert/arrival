Map

See the full
picture, live.

Pop open the map to see exactly where traffic is backing up. It floats right over your workspace — no app-switching required.

  • Floating map window you can detach and move around
  • Works with Apple Maps out of the box — no API key needed
  • Add a free Mapbox key for per-segment congestion coloring
  • Your route drawn right on the map
Incidents

Know what's
causing the hold-up.

Arrival shows you what's happening on your route — whether it's a fender-bender, a road closure, or just a slow stretch you can wait out. Requires a Mapbox API key.

  • Accidents, construction, and road closures
  • Severity shown so you know what's worth worrying about
  • Refreshes automatically along with your route data

Traffic has a mood. Arrival tells you what it is.

Each route gets a plain-English status — not just a number — so you know what you're dealing with at a glance.

"All clear, captain"

Homebrew

Install via Homebrew Cask for easy updates.

Privacy-first

Your commute, your business.

Arrival collects anonymous usage analytics via TelemetryDeck to help us improve — things like "app launched" and "route fetched." No personal data, no addresses, no location info. Ever. We use Sentry for crash reporting so bugs get fixed fast. All your settings and addresses stay on your Mac.
